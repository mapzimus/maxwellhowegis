How to play

BUG WARS

A backyard real-time strategy game. Out-economy, out-build, and out-fight a rival ant colony.

Gather — drag-select workers, right-click Food / Mud / Honeydew to mine. They keep at it until you move them.
Build & Train — spend Mud on buildings, Food to train units. Counters matter: infantry > skirmisher > siege > buildings, and skirmishers shoot down flyers.
Win — destroy the enemy base before they destroy yours. You'll get a warning when they march.
Play as — you'll face the other faction
