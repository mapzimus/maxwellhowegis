Data comes from Massachusetts DESE, the U.S. Census / ACS, and IPEDS. Full credits live under “Sources / Credits / About” in the panel.

MCAS
Massachusetts' statewide student test (grades 3–8 and 10).
% Meeting / Exceeding
Share of students at or above grade level on MCAS.
Growth (SGP)
How much students improve year-to-year — 1 to 99, about 50 is a typical year — regardless of starting scores.
Accountability percentile
DESE's overall ranking of a school vs. others statewide, 1 (low) to 99 (high).
Per-pupil $
Dollars spent per student, per year.
English Learner
Students still learning English.
High Needs
DESE's combined group: low-income, English-learner, and/or students with disabilities.
Chronic absence
Share of students who miss 10% or more of school days.
