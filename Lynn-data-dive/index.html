Lynn Data Dive

Built 2026 · Original analysis · Maxwell Howe · Updated when DESE releases new data

An interactive, public exploration of every dataset that touches Lynn English High School and the 26 Massachusetts Gateway Cities. MCAS, enrollment, demographics, attendance, discipline, finance, teacher workforce, AP, graduation, college outcomes, community context — pulled from MA DESE, US Census ACS, and original geospatial research, integrated in one place.

DESE publishes these in separate Power BI dashboards and downloadable CSVs. Putting them together at the school level is the difference between a stack of separate facts and a coherent picture of one school. A correlation lab on top lets you cross-reference any two metrics across all 26 gateway-city high schools.

The dashboard also embeds my prior chronic absenteeism capstone as its Catchment Research page, with all underlying student address data kept private.
