Who I Am

I'm Maxwell Howe. I have a Master's degree in Geo-Information Science from Salem State University (2025).

My graduate work covered remote sensing with Landsat/Sentinel imagery, spatial database design with PostGIS and SQL, computer cartography, digital image processing in TerrSet, quantitative methods, and geoprocessing automation with Python and R. I've worked on projects for municipal governments, non-profits, and my own research interests.

I also built Mapzimus — a scoring and idea management tool for planning infographic maps. It's how I organize and prioritize map topics before I build them.

I'm looking for my next role in GIS, remote sensing, or spatial data science.
